• ?   Length of the program?
  • Eight consecutive semesters spanning 31 months.
  • ?   Degree to be rewarded?
  • Upon successful completion of the Rocky Mountain University of Health Professions Physician Assistant Program (RMUoHP-PAP), students will be awarded a Master of Physician Assistant Studies (MPAS) degree.
  • ?   Advanced placement?
  • {% include advanced-placement.md %}
  • ?   Drug screening?
  • Drug screening will be performed on all students during at the didactic period prior to the onset of the first phase of full-time clinical learning experiences and then may be repeated one or more times thereafter at unpredictable times.
  • ?   Seats available for each class?
  • Our goal is to seat 46 students for the inaugural class and 50 students for each class annually thereafter. However, we will only seat as many as we feel confident we can accommodate with strong clinical rotations.
  • ?   Prereqs before applying?
  • No. However, as described on the Prerequisite Courses page, those applicants who have outstanding prerequisite coursework at the time of application must have a feasible plan documented for completing remaining prerequisites prior to the program start date. Furthermore, applicants offered seats contingent upon successful completion of outstanding prerequisites must complete each remaining course with a grade of “B” or better.
